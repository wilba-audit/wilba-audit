## Primary Business: WILBA (wilba.ai)

**What it is:** An AI automation and content generation agency. WILBA helps businesses — primarily coaches, service providers, and hospitality/retreat operators — save time, automate repetitive work, and build content systems powered by AI.

**The model:**
- Jess leads sales, strategy, audits, and client relationships
- A US-based developer partner handles technical fulfillment (contractor model)
- Revenue comes from setup/onboarding fees + monthly retainers

**Two core service lines:**

### 1. Content Generation Machine
A done-with-you AI content system. Built in Lovable. The workflow:
- Perplexity searches for trending topics/articles
- AI generates a custom script (client edits if needed)
- Script publishes to ElevenLabs (voice) → HeyGen (avatar video) → CreatorMate (final video)
- Output: consistent short-form video content at scale

**Offer model:** Custom setup fee (per client) + monthly subscription to run and maintain the system. Clients can run it themselves after onboarding, or keep Jess's team managing it.

**Primary target:** Life coaches, business coaches, online educators — people who need consistent content but don't have time to create it manually.

### 2. AI Automation Audit & Implementation
Jess conducts free business audits to identify the highest-ROI automation opportunities. She maps pain points, time sinks, and manual processes, then proposes AI/automation solutions. Implementation is quoted per project and handled by her developer.

**Pitch:** "If you can dream it automated, we can build it. We find where you're bleeding time and fix it."

**Target sectors:** Coaches, real estate, retreats/hospitality, surf resorts, education businesses.

**Brand personality:** Modern, clever, witty, down-to-earth. Think Matthew McConaughey meets The Dirt Company — confident, cheeky, not corporate.

**Brand positioning and voice guide:** See `outputs/brand/brand-positioning.md`
**Audience intelligence:** See `outputs/brand/social-media-intelligence.md`
