# /content-week — Batch a Whole Week in One Session

> The leverage move. Plan and pre-write 7 days of content at once so posting is just uploading.

## Instructions

Produce a full week of content, built to be recorded/designed in one batch.

### Step 1 — Gather raw material
- Launch **trend-scout** for this week's freshest angles from The Rundown AI.
- Read the 30-day calendar in `outputs/brand/social-media-intelligence.md` to see what slot we're up to and keep pillar balance.

### Step 2 — Plan the week
Lay out a 7-day plan balancing the 4 pillars (Revenue Leak / Behind the AI / Social Proof / Jess's World) and both platforms. Follow the intelligence doc cadence: ~4-5 Reels, 2-3 carousels, 1 YouTube long-form, Shorts feeding from it. Assign each day a pillar, segment, format, and a one-line concept. Show Jess this plan and get a quick thumbs-up before writing everything.

### Step 3 — Write the batch
For each day, launch **hook-writer** to produce the copy (reels get hooks+script+caption; carousels get slides; YouTube gets title+outline). Keep them varied — don't let every hook use the same formula.

### Step 4 — Queue the visuals
For the hero pieces (YouTube thumbnail, carousel covers, any faceless reels), launch **visual-producer** to generate them. For simpler talking-head reels, just give the shoot note — Jess films those herself.

### Step 5 — Deliver the week
Save to `outputs/content/weeks/YYYY-Www-plan.md`:
- The 7-day table (day / platform / pillar / segment / concept / status)
- Each day's full copy
- Visuals generated + ids
- A **batch shoot list** — group all talking-head reels to film in one 90-min session, all carousels to design together

Chat summary: "Your week is planned and written. Batch-film the [N] reels in one sitting, [N] visuals already made. Here's the shoot list."

Keep everything on-brand. This is the command that buys Jess her time back.
