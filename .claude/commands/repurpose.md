# /repurpose [video URL or script] — One Piece Becomes Six

> Record once, publish everywhere. Turn one YouTube video or script into a Reel, Short, carousel, Story series, and email.

## Instructions

**Input:** `$ARGUMENTS` — a YouTube URL, a path to a script/transcript in `outputs/`, or pasted text. If empty, ask Jess what she wants to repurpose.

Launch the **repurposer** agent with the input. It returns a full repurposing pack:
1. The long-form (as-is / tightened)
2. 1 Short/Reel clip (if a YouTube URL, it actually cuts the clip via Higgsfield personal clipper)
3. 1 carousel (framework laid out slide-by-slide)
4. 1 Story series (3-5 frames + poll/DM CTA)
5. 1 standalone Reel from the best quote
6. 1 email / LinkedIn post

Each piece stands alone, is reshaped for its platform, and ends with the one CTA. Save the pack to `outputs/content/repurpose-[slug].md` and give Jess a suggested week-long posting order.

Run the banned-phrases check across everything before delivering.
