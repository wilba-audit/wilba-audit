# /outreach — Today's Warm Outreach, Written For You

> The cash engine's daily fuel. Get 10-12 personalised messages, ready to send.

## Instructions

Goal: hand Jess a batch of ready-to-send outreach so her only job is to hit send.

### Step 1 — Who to reach today
Read `outputs/wilba-pipeline.md`:
- Anyone due a **follow-up** (audit sent but no reply, call not booked, proposal pending) — these come first, follow-up is where deals close.
- Then **new warm contacts** from the list (real estate first per the sprint plan).
If the list is thin, tell Jess and help her add names (segment by segment).

### Step 2 — Write them
Launch the **outreach-writer** agent for each prospect (or batch by segment). Get back ready-to-send messages — DMs and emails — each with one CTA (the free audit or a quick chat), in Jess's voice, matched to their segment.

### Step 3 — Deliver
Present the batch as a simple send list:
- **[Name] · [segment] · [DM/email]** → the message, ready to copy
- Mark which are follow-ups vs first touches.

Then update `outputs/wilba-pipeline.md` — set each to "Outreach sent" / "Follow-up sent" with today's date.

End with the day's outreach target (10-12 touches) and a one-line nudge: "Hit send on these, then log any replies with `/pipeline`."

Keep it fast — this should take Jess 15 minutes to send a full day's outreach.
