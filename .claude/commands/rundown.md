# /rundown — Today's AI News, Translated Into WILBA Angles

> Just the angles. No production. 60 seconds to "here's what I'd post about today."

## Instructions

Launch the **trend-scout** agent. It pulls today's The Rundown AI email from Gmail, filters out the noise, and returns 2-3 content angles reframed for WILBA's audience — each with a pillar, a segment, a written hook, and a format call, ranked best-first.

Present the results as a short, skimmable list Jess can scan on her phone. End with the **#1 pick of the day** and one line on why.

Then offer the obvious next step: **"Want me to write and produce the #1 pick? Just say `/make-reel` or `/content`."**

Do not produce copy or visuals here — this command is the fast scan only.
