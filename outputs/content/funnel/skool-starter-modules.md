# The AIOS Builders — Starter Modules
## The first 3 classroom modules, scripted. Paste into Skool so day-one members get a real win.

**Voice:** Jess — plain, warm, "if I can, you can." No jargon. Every module ends with the member having *built something*.
**Format:** each module = a short intro + steps + a "your win" action. You can film these as short lessons or leave them as written guides to start.

---

## 📦 Module 1 — The Starter Setup (your AIOS foundation)
*Goal: member installs Claude Code and gives their AI the context of their business. No code.*

**Intro (script):**
> Welcome to your first build. Before we make anything clever, we do the one thing 90% of people skip — and it's the reason their AI stays dumb. We give it context. We tell it, once, everything about your business. Do this and every single thing you build after is smart about who you are. Let's go.

**Steps:**
1. Install Claude Code (link + 2-min walkthrough).
2. Create a `CLAUDE.md` file — your business's brain. (Template provided.)
3. Fill in 5 things: what your business does, who you serve, how you talk, what you sell, your goals.
4. Say hi to your AI and watch it already understand your business.

**Your win:** "Your AI now knows your business. You just built the foundation of an AIOS — most people never do this."

**Provide:** a `CLAUDE.md` starter template (I'll write it — a generic version of Jess's own).

## 🛠 Module 2 — Your First Skill (automate one boring task)
*Goal: member builds one skill that automates a task they do every week. No code.*

**Intro (script):**
> Now the fun part. We're going to take one thing you do every week — that you slightly hate — and hand it to your AI forever. Not the impressive stuff. The boring, repetitive thing. That's where the time comes back.

**Steps:**
1. Pick your task (prompt: "what do you do every week that's repetitive and rule-based?").
2. Describe it to Claude in plain English — what goes in, what should come out.
3. Save it as a skill (a simple command file — template provided).
4. Run it. Watch it work. Run it again tomorrow without re-explaining.

**Your win:** "You just built an employee that only needed training once. That's a skill. Now you can build a hundred."

**Provide:** a skill/command template + 3 worked examples (a proposal drafter, an email summariser, a weekly-report builder).

## 📣 Module 3 — The Content Engine (never face a blank page)
*Goal: member builds a version of Jess's content system for their own brand.*

**Intro (script):**
> This is the exact system I use so I never stare at a blank page. It reads what's happening in my world every morning and drafts content in my voice. We're going to build yours.

**Steps:**
1. Point your AI at the sources you already read (a newsletter, a feed).
2. Give it your voice (paste 3 things you've written that sound like you).
3. Build the "give me today's post" skill.
4. Run it — get your first AI-drafted, on-brand post.

**Your win:** "You now have a content machine. Direct it; don't write from scratch. This is how you stay consistent without living in your phone."

**Provide:** the trend-scout + hook-writer approach, simplified into a member-buildable version.

## The weekly Build-With-Me format (live sessions)
Each Wednesday, ~30 min:
1. **Pick a real task** from a member (posted in the community that week).
2. **Build it live** — describe → build → run → it works.
3. **Members build alongside** for their own business.
4. **Homework:** post your version in "Show Your AIOS."

Rotating themes: sales follow-up bot · client onboarding · a reporting dashboard · an inbox triager · a proposal generator. (Every one is also a "Steal This Skill" reel for the feed — the community and the content feed each other.)
