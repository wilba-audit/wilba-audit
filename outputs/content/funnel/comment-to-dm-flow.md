# Comment-to-DM Funnel — Setup + Copy
## The mechanic that turns Reel comments into leads on autopilot

**Keyword:** `SKILLS`
**Tool:** Manychat (free tier works to start) — or Instagram's native "keyword → DM" if you prefer no third-party tool.
**Goal:** someone comments SKILLS → they instantly get the skills sheet in their DMs → we capture their email → they enter the nurture → they join the Skool.

> This must be LIVE before you post any reel with a "comment SKILLS" CTA — otherwise the leads pile up in your comments and go nowhere.

---

## The flow (what happens)

```
Viewer comments "SKILLS" on your reel
        ▼
[Auto public reply]  "Sent! Check your DMs 📩"
        ▼
[Auto DM 1]  delivers the skills sheet link + warm hello
        ▼
[Auto DM 2]  asks a quick question (starts a real conversation + qualifies)
        ▼
[Email capture]  link to the sheet gates the email → into nurture sequence
```

## The copy (paste straight into Manychat)

**Public comment auto-reply** (keep it human, vary it):
> "Sent it through — check your DMs 📩"
> alt: "On its way to your DMs 👀"
> alt: "Done! It's in your messages 🙌"

**Auto DM 1 — deliver the value immediately:**
> Hey! Here's the skills sheet I promised — every AI skill I use to run my business, and how to steal them 👇
>
> 👉 [skills sheet link]
>
> I built all of it without knowing how to code. If I can, you genuinely can.

**Auto DM 2 — start a conversation (send ~1 min later):**
> Quick one so I can point you at the right stuff — are you building AI for **your own business** or a **client's**?
>
> (Reply 1 or 2 — I read these 👀)

> *Why this matters:* a reply turns Instagram's algorithm on for that person (it now treats you as connected), and it tells you who your audience actually is. Tag them in Manychat by answer.

**Auto DM 3 — the soft invite (send after they reply, or 1 day later):**
> Love it. If you want to actually *build* these (not just read about them), I run a small community — The AIOS Builders — where we build a real skill together every week.
>
> Founding members lock in the lowest price it'll ever be: [Skool link]
>
> No pressure — the sheet's yours to keep either way 🤙

## Email capture (important — don't skip)

Instagram DMs are rented land. Email is yours. Two ways to capture:

**Option A (simplest):** the skills-sheet link goes to a landing page / Notion with a "get the printable version + future skills" email opt-in before the full sheet.

**Option B (higher friction, higher intent):** DM 1 links to a short form (email → sheet). Fewer opt-ins, warmer leads.

Start with **A**. Whatever email tool you use (Kajabi, ConvertKit, MailerLite) — that's where the nurture sequence in `email-nurture.md` runs.

## Manychat setup — the exact clicks

1. Create a free Manychat account → connect your Instagram (must be a Business/Creator account).
2. **Automation → New → Instagram → Comments.**
3. Trigger: **specific keyword = `SKILLS`** (also add lowercase "skills"). Apply to **all posts** (or select posts).
4. Action 1: **auto-reply to comment** with the public reply copy above.
5. Action 2: **send DM** → paste DM 1 (with the sheet link button).
6. Add a **delay (1 min) → DM 2**.
7. Add a **condition on their reply** → tag + send DM 3.
8. **Test it yourself** from a second account before going live. Comment SKILLS, confirm the DM lands.

**Do-it-in-10-minutes version:** just steps 1–5. The conversation layer (6–8) can come after your first reel proves it works.
