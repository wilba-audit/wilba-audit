# Email Nurture — Skills Sheet → Skool
## 5 emails from opt-in to founding member. In Jess's voice.

**Trigger:** someone gives their email to get the skills sheet.
**Goal:** deliver value, build trust, invite to The AIOS Builders.
**Cadence:** Day 0, 1, 3, 5, 7. Keep them short — these get read on phones.
**Voice:** plain, warm, confident. No hype. One idea each.

---

### Email 1 — Day 0 · Deliver
**Subject:** Here's your skills sheet 🛠
**Body:**
Hey — here it is, as promised:

👉 [Skills sheet link]

Every one of those skills runs my business right now. And I built them all without knowing how to code — just by describing what I wanted in plain English.

Have a read. Steal whatever's useful. Tomorrow I'll show you the one I'd build first if I were starting today.

— Jess

P.S. Reply and tell me: what's the one task in your business you'd most love AI to just handle? I read every reply.

---

### Email 2 — Day 1 · The one to build first
**Subject:** If I were starting today, I'd build this first
**Body:**
Quick one.

Everyone wants to build the impressive stuff first. Don't. Build the *boring* thing you do every single day — the report, the follow-up, the morning check.

For me it was content. I was drowning in it. So I built an AI that reads the news and drafts my posts before I've had coffee. That one skill gave me back hours a week.

Pick your version of that. The thing you do daily, hate slightly, and could describe to a smart assistant in two sentences. That's your first skill.

Tomorrow: the mistake that keeps founders stuck (I made it for a year).

— Jess

---

### Email 3 — Day 3 · The belief shift
**Subject:** Stop learning to code. Seriously.
**Body:**
The advice to "learn to code" is now like learning to build an engine so you can drive to work.

You don't need it. The skill that matters in 2026 isn't code — it's **clarity**. Knowing exactly what you want your business to do. And you already have that, because you run the business.

I'm not technical. I never became technical. I just got very good at describing what I wanted — and let the AI build it. That's the whole unlock.

If that clicks for you, you're exactly who I built my community for. More on that tomorrow.

— Jess
