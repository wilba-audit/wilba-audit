## 📣 The Content Engine
*Turns daily AI news into finished, on-brand content — so I never face a blank page.*

| Skill | What it does |
|---|---|
| **`/content`** | The whole morning loop: reads the day's AI news → finds the angles my audience cares about → writes me a post in my voice → makes the visuals. One command. |
| **Trend Scout** | An agent that reads my newsletters every morning and surfaces the 2–3 ideas actually worth posting about. |
| **Hook Writer** | Turns any idea into a finished script + caption, in my exact voice, checked against my brand rules. |
| **Visual Producer** | Generates thumbnails, carousels, and b-roll — on-brand — and predicts how a video will perform *before* I post it. |
| **Repurposer** | One video → a Reel, a Short, a carousel, a Story, and an email. Record once, post everywhere. |

**Steal the idea:** point your AI at the sources you already read, give it your voice, and let it draft. You direct; it produces.

## 💸 The Audit Machine
*My best salesperson — and it's software.*

Someone fills out a form on my site. 60 seconds later they get a **personalised report** — their exact numbers, their roadmap — in their inbox. No human touches it. It reads the answers, does the maths, writes the report, sends it. While I sleep.

**Steal the idea:** any time you send the "same kind of document" to different people (quotes, reports, proposals), that's a skill waiting to be built.

## 🌅 The Daily Brief
*Hands a messy business to AI and gets back a clean morning.*

For a client, I fed the AI everything — bookings, team, the daily chaos — and asked for a brief every morning. Now, before anyone's awake, it pulls it all together: who's arriving, who's leaving, what needs to happen. Sent automatically at 6am.

**Steal the idea:** whatever you check across 5 tabs every morning, an agent can check for you and hand you the summary.
