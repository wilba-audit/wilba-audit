## The Problem

Running a surf villa in West Sumbawa means juggling bookings from five different places — Booking Layer, OTAs through Channex, World Surfaris packages, Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, and walk-ins. Every morning, someone has to check all of them, piece together who's arriving, who's leaving, what transfers need to happen, and who's eating breakfast.

Meanwhile, guest inquiries hit the inbox in English, French, Spanish, Indonesian. Replying in the guest's language takes time the team doesn't have.

This is the operator trap. Time that should go to running a great property goes to admin.

**We fix it.**

---

## The Solution

Two AI automations that run while you sleep:

### 1. The Daily Ops Brief

Every morning at 6am, your team's Gmail inbox receives one email. It covers everything:

- **Checking in today** — guest names, rooms, packages, arrival times, transfer details
- **Checking out today** — who's leaving, room turnover required, departures
- **Transfers today** — every pickup and drop, in time order
- **Currently in house** — full guest list with packages and any special requests
- **Next 48 hours** — upcoming arrivals so you can prepare
- **Flags** — anything missing, unusual, or needing attention

Zero system-checking. Zero piecing together from five tabs. One email, everything you need.

### 2. The AI Receptionist (Multi-Language Email Responder)

When a guest emails in French, they get a reply in French. Spanish — Spanish. Indonesian — Indonesian. English — English. Within minutes, not hours.

The AI reads every incoming email, understands what the guest is asking, and drafts a warm, professional reply in their language. You review and send, or for simple questions (WiFi, check-in time, what's included) — it auto-sends and you never see it.

Your inbox is handled. Your guests feel looked after. Your team focuses on the property, not the inbox.
