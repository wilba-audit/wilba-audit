## Part 1 — What an AIOS for Baha Baha actually is

Not just the two automations already scoped (daily brief + email responder). Those become *modules* inside a bigger system. The AIOS wraps his whole business in five layers — each one independently valuable, each one separately billable.

| Layer | What it means for Baha Baha | Example |
|-------|------------------------------|---------|
| **1. Context** | The AI understands the villa — rooms, packages, rates, policies, team, brand voice, guest types, seasonality | Ask it "what's our cancellation policy in French?" and it knows |
| **2. Data** | Live numbers pulled daily from his real systems | Occupancy, revenue, ADR, F&B sales, OTA vs direct mix — one dashboard |
| **3. Intelligence** | The AI watches everything and hands him a daily brief | 6am: arrivals, departures, transfers, revenue, guest messages needing attention |
| **4. Automate** | Score every recurring task, automate them one by one | Email responder, WhatsApp replies, review requests, transfer scheduling, pre-arrival sequences, OTA message sync |
| **5. Build** | Freed bandwidth → growth | More direct bookings (cut OTA commissions), retreats, occupancy optimisation |

**Data sources already known:** Booking Layer (PMS), Channex (OTAs), World Surfaris (packages), Mockapos (POS/F&B), Jurnal.co.id (accounting), Gmail, Squarespace, WhatsApp, Instagram.

## Part 2 — The discovery questions (run these with Sean)

The goal: understand the business well enough to know what to build, what it's worth, and where he's bleeding time. Grouped so you can work through them naturally in conversation.

### A. Business & revenue model
- What are all the ways the business makes money? (rooms, surf packages, F&B, transfers, retreats, gear hire, events?)
- How many rooms/villas, and what are the room types + rack rates?
- What packages exist — accommodation only, surf-included, meals, combos? Exact names?
- What's your average occupancy? Peak vs low season months?
- Rough monthly revenue, and how is it split across those streams?
- What % of bookings are direct vs OTA? What commission do the OTAs take?
- What would one more booking a week, direct, be worth to you?

### B. The numbers he watches (Data layer)
- What numbers do you check, and how often — daily, weekly, monthly?
- Where do those numbers live right now? How long does it take to pull them together?
- What do you *wish* you could see at a glance but can't?
- Who else needs to see the numbers?

### C. Operations & daily workflow (Intelligence + Automate)
- Walk me through a normal morning — who checks what, in what order?
- Who's arriving/leaving today: how do you figure that out currently?
- How are airport pickups and surf-spot transfers scheduled and tracked?
- Housekeeping, F&B, maintenance — how do those get coordinated?
- What falls through the cracks most often?

### D. Guests & communication
- Who are your guests — where from, what languages?
- How do they find and book you? (website, OTA, IG, WhatsApp, word of mouth)
- Which channels do they message you on? Which is busiest?
- What are the 10 most common questions guests ask?
- How fast do you reply now, and who handles it?
- Special requests / dietary needs — where are those tracked?
- How do you currently ask for reviews? Do you follow up after a stay?

### E. Team
- How many people, what roles? Who's local, who's remote?
- English levels across the team?
- What takes up most of your team's time each day?
- What do they most dislike doing / what's most error-prone?
- What only *you* can do right now that you wish someone (or something) else could?

### F. Tech & data access
- For each system (Booking Layer, Channex, Mockapos, Jurnal, Gmail) — do you have admin access + API access?
- What talks to what already? What's still manual copy-paste?
- How do World Surfaris bookings arrive — email, PDF, portal?
- Is WhatsApp on a business account (API-capable) or a personal phone?

### G. Pain points & the operator trap
- Where does your time actually go each week?
- What's the thing that, if it just handled itself, would change your life?
- What keeps you tied to the property / stops you stepping away?
- What worries you about the business right now?

### H. Vision & goals (this is where the value is)
- Where do you want the business in 12 months? In 3 years?
- Do you want to grow occupancy, add retreats, go more direct, open more villas?
- What do you want to STOP doing personally?
- What does "the business runs without me" look like for you?
- If we could build anything, what would you dream up?

### I. The working relationship & commercials
- Who makes the decisions / holds the budget?
- What have you spent on tools or help before?
- How hands-on do you want to be vs. "just make it work"?
- What's your timeline — what's urgent vs. nice-to-have?

## Part 3 — How to structure it so you get paid (and still "work together")

The trap to avoid: endless free consulting because you're friends and it's fun. Structure it as a real engagement from day one.

**Recommended path:**

1. **Paid Discovery + AIOS Roadmap** — a paid workshop (run the questions above) → you deliver a written AIOS roadmap: the 5 layers mapped to his business, modules prioritised, a task-automation scoreboard, and pricing per module. *Charge for this* (e.g. USD $300–500). It's real value, it filters serious intent, and it de-risks the build for both of you.
2. **Foundation (Context + Data)** — set up the context layer + a live data dashboard. Setup fee + monthly.
3. **Module-by-module build** — each automation is its own mini-project with its own setup + retainer. Start with the two already built (daily brief + email responder) since they're ready — quickest win, fastest cash.
4. **Ongoing retainer** — monitoring, upkeep, quarterly optimisation. Recurring revenue.

**Framing for Sean:** "We build this together — you're the expert on your business, I build the system around it. We do it one layer at a time so you see value (and I earn) at each step, rather than one big scary project." That's honest, collaborative, AND gets you paid at every stage.

**Money already on the table:** the two scoped automations = $1,800 USD setup + $300/month. The full AIOS is bigger — the roadmap workshop is how you expand the deal without over-promising.
