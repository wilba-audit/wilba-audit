# Diagram Engine

**For you if: you want to visualise your AIOS architecture, create system diagrams for clients, or turn any process into a professional diagram.**

---

## What This Is

Diagram Engine installs D2 — a modern diagramming language — and gives AI everything it needs to create professional architecture diagrams autonomously. AI writes the source code, renders it to PNG, visually validates the result, and iterates until it looks right.

Includes 5 design patterns (master overview, data pipeline, automation schedule, command system, education funnel) and a render script that handles the build process. You describe what you want in plain English. AI builds it.

---

## Why This Matters

Diagrams make the complex simple. Whether you're mapping your own business processes, creating a client deliverable, or documenting a system architecture — having professional diagrams on demand is a superpower. No Figma, no Miro, no wrestling with drag-and-drop tools. Just describe what you want.

Particularly useful for client-facing work — a clean architecture diagram communicates more value than any amount of text.
