# Content Pipeline

**For you if: content creation is on your Task Audit. You create YouTube videos, LinkedIn posts, newsletters, or any regular content and want to systematise ideation, development, and scheduling.**

---

## What This Is

Content Pipeline is a full content lifecycle system. It starts with an interactive Brand & Content Workshop where AI interviews you about your brand, audience, content pillars, and strategy — then builds a personalised content engine around your answers.

Three commands handle the entire lifecycle:

- **/capture** — Quick idea capture. See something interesting? Hear something in a meeting? Just tell AI — it classifies the idea by channel, format, and content pillar, then stores it in your pipeline database.
- **/develop** — Full concept development. Takes a captured idea and develops it into a complete concept — strategic positioning, title options, thumbnail concepts, hook strategies. Uses a 7-day context window so every concept is grounded in what's actually happening in your business.
- **/schedule** — Batch scheduling. Shows your developed ideas ranked by priority. Pick what to schedule, set publish dates, and the system calculates film-by dates.

Supports YouTube and LinkedIn out of the box. Optional Notion sync for visual pipeline management.

---

## Why This Matters

Content creation is one of the highest-effort recurring tasks for most business owners. The pipeline from idea to published piece involves ideation, research, scripting, production, and scheduling — all manual, all time-consuming.

Content Pipeline automates the thinking and organising. You still create the content — but the strategy, development, and scheduling happen automatically.
