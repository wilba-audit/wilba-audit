# IntelOS

**Your AI gets ears. Every meeting, every message — collected, classified, and searchable forever.**

---

## What This Is

IntelOS is the intelligence collection layer. It connects to your meeting recording service (Fireflies, Fathom, or a custom recorder) and your messaging platforms to pull meeting transcripts and messages into your database. Meetings get classified by department. Everything becomes queryable. Automated collection keeps data fresh.

This module is about collection and storage — gathering the raw intelligence. The analysis and synthesis happens in the Daily Brief module.

---

## Why This Matters

Right now, information from meetings and messages disappears into the void. You had an important call last Tuesday — what was decided? Someone mentioned a key insight three days ago — what was it? Without IntelOS, you either remember it or it's gone.

With IntelOS, every meeting and every message is captured, stored, and searchable. Your Daily Brief can't synthesise what it can't see — IntelOS gives it eyes and ears across your entire organisation.

- Meeting recordings collected from Fireflies, Fathom, or custom recorder
- Messages stored with granular searchability
- Meeting classification by department
- Query helpers for searching across meetings and messages
- Automated collection on a schedule
