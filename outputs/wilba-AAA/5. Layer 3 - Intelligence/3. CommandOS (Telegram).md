# CommandOS (Telegram)

**Your AIOS goes in your pocket. Text, voice notes, photos — all processed with full workspace context, accessible from your phone 24/7.**

---

## What This Is

CommandOS is a Telegram bot connected to persistent AI agent sessions. You message it from your phone — text, voice notes, photos — and it responds with the full context of your AIOS workspace. It has session management, optional voice transcription, and can generate charts, PDFs, and detailed reports. Your AI accessible from anywhere.

---

## Why This Matters

Your AIOS is only as useful as your ability to access it. Right now, you need to be at your computer to interact with AI. CommandOS changes that — your AI lives in your pocket.

Make decisions on the go. Ask questions while walking the dog. Check metrics from the airport. Get a full analysis delivered while you're at dinner. Send a voice note saying *"Give me a summary of last week's numbers"* and get an intelligent response in seconds.

This is how you run your business from your phone — and it's where the Away-From-Desk Autonomy KPI starts climbing.
