## What This Is

The Daily Brief is an adaptive morning intelligence report that synthesises your metrics (from DataOS), your meetings and messages (from IntelOS), and your business context (from ContextOS) into a single comprehensive analysis. It produces a dashboard image, a narrative brief, key signals, and strategic recommendations — delivered to Telegram every morning before breakfast.

Three presets: Solo Operator, Small Team, Agency — each with customisable sections and depth. It adapts to YOUR specific business. And the Telegram topic is interactive — reply to drill into anything.
