# The Five Principles

**How we think while we build.**

These aren't rules. They're mental models — the principles that guide every AIOS we build at WILBA.

---

## 1. Just Ask

**If you can describe it in plain English, it can be built.**

The single most powerful shift: believe that anything is automatable, and then just ask. Don't assume it's too complex. Don't assume you need a developer. Don't assume it's impossible. The person who asks for the impossible gets it — while the person who assumes it's impossible keeps doing it manually.

*Example:* "I want something that knows everything about my business so that when I talk to it about strategy, it can think about all the different pieces." — That's not a fantasy. That's Layer 1.

---

## 2. Talk, Don't Type

**Voice-first interaction.**

You're the business owner. You give instructions. You don't sit in a chat box typing carefully formatted messages. Hold your function key, speak for 60 seconds, and let AI format it. 3x faster than typing.

---

## 3. Layers, Not Leaps

**One layer at a time. Each independently valuable.**

You don't need to build all five layers to see results. Layer 1 alone makes every AI interaction dramatically better. Layer 2 gives you a real-time dashboard. Layer 3 gives you a morning brief. Each one stands on its own.

---

## 4. Build for Scale & Security

**Human-in-the-loop by default. Your data stays yours.**

We never build fully autonomous systems on day one. Every automation starts with human review. Drafts go to your inbox for approval. Responses get checked before sending. As trust builds, you dial up autonomy. Your data stays local, your credentials stay secure, and nothing happens without your knowledge.

---

## 5. Borrow Before You Build

**80% proven modules, 20% custom.**

Before building anything from scratch, we check what's already been built, tested, and refined. Pre-built modules solve most common problems in minutes rather than days. Only after exhausting proven solutions do we build custom.

---

## How WILBA Applies These

Every AIOS we build follows these five principles. We explain what we're doing in plain English before we do it. We build in layers so you see value from week one. We start with human review and graduate to automation. And we never build from scratch when a proven solution exists.
