# Your Roadmap

**Here's exactly what gets built, in what order, and what you'll have at each stage.**

---

## The Journey

The path is simple:

1. **Set up your layers** — Context, Data, Intelligence (Layers 1-3)
2. **Audit your tasks** — Map where your time actually goes (Layer 4)
3. **Automate from the library** — Proven modules solve most common problems
4. **Build custom** — Whatever's left gets designed and built bespoke (Layer 5)

Each step has a clear outcome. Each layer has a clear win. You always know where you are and what's next.
