# Your Three KPIs

**How to know if your AIOS is actually working.**

Not vanity metrics. Not "how many automations did I build." Three specific numbers that tell you whether you're escaping The Operator Trap — or just rearranging the furniture.

---

## KPI 1: Away-From-Desk Autonomy

**How many hours per day can you step away and nothing falls apart?**

This is the north star. When you start, it's zero — everything requires you at your desk. As layers get added, this number climbs.

The goal isn't "AI does everything." The goal is freedom. Taking the morning off to go to the gym. Leaving your laptop at home for a weekend trip. Running your business from your phone while you're out living your life.

**Test it:** Take a Friday off. Leave your laptop closed. Can you make critical decisions from your phone in 15 minutes? Can the rest wait until Monday? If yes — your AIOS is working.

- Starting point: 0 hours
- Target: Your business runs while you sleep, travel, or take the morning off
- This KPI starts climbing at Layer 3

---

## KPI 2: Task Automation %

**What percentage of recurring tasks are automated?**

List everything you do across your business. Every task, every recurring responsibility. Then cross them off, one by one, as your AIOS takes them over.

This is your personal scoreboard. Every item ticked off is bandwidth permanently recovered.

- Starting point: 0%
- Target: 80%+ of recurring tasks automated or heavily augmented

---

## KPI 3: Revenue Per Employee

**Total revenue divided by team members.**

Not bigger companies — leaner, faster, more profitable ones. An AIOS lets a team of 3 operate like a team of 15. Revenue per employee is the clearest signal that your AI is creating real leverage.

- Starting point: Current revenue / current team size
- Target: 2-5x improvement as automations replace manual work
