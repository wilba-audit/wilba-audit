# Client Onboarding Process

**How we get your AIOS up and running.**

---

## What's Covered in Onboarding

Your onboarding with WILBA follows a structured process designed to get you from zero to a working AI Operating System as quickly as possible:

1. **Discovery Call** — We learn about your business, your pain points, and your goals
2. **Tech Stack Audit** — We map every tool you use and identify integration points
3. **Context Build** — We build your AI's knowledge base from your business documents, strategy, and data
4. **Module Selection** — We match your needs to proven modules from our library
5. **Build & Configure** — We install, customise, and connect everything
6. **Test & Validate** — We test with your real data before anything goes live
7. **Handover & Training** — We show you how to use your new system

---

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Discovery Call (30 minutes)
We get on a call and you tell us about your business. What do you do? Who's on your team? What takes up most of your time? What do you wish you didn't have to do? No preparation needed — just talk.

### 2. Tech Stack Audit (async)
We map every tool, platform, and system you use: your CRM, your booking system, your email, your analytics, your accounting software. This tells us what we can connect to and what data is available.

### 3. Context Build (1-2 days)
We build your AI's brain. Business info, personal info, strategy, current data — all structured so your AI starts every conversation fully informed about YOUR business.

### 4. Module Selection (collaborative)
Based on your discovery call and tech audit, we recommend which modules to install. You approve the plan before we start building.

### 5. Build & Configure (1-2 weeks)
We install each module, connect your data sources, configure your automations, and customise everything for your specific workflows.

### 6. Test & Validate (2-3 days)
Nothing goes live without testing. We verify every automation with your real data. You review outputs before we flip the switch.

### 7. Handover & Training (1 hour)
We walk you through your new system. How to read your daily brief. How to talk to your AI. How to request changes. You leave with confidence that it works — and knowledge of how to use it.
