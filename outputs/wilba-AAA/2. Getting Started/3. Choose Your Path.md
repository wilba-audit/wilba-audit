# Choose Your Path

**Two ways to work with WILBA. Pick the one that fits how you operate.**

---

## The Done-For-You Path

**For business owners who want results without getting technical.**

We handle everything. You tell us about your business, we build your AIOS, and you start seeing results. You never touch code, never configure settings, never troubleshoot.

**Who this is for:**
- You're a business owner focused on running YOUR business
- You want zero friction — just get it working
- Technical stuff isn't your thing (and you don't want it to be)
- You want to see results, not learn a new skill

**What you get:**
- Full AIOS built and deployed by WILBA
- All five layers configured for your business
- Ongoing support and maintenance
- Training on how to use your system (not how to build it)

---

## The Guided Path

**For business owners who want to understand the system and grow with it.**

We build the foundation together. You learn how the pieces connect, how to talk to your AI, and how to request new automations as your needs evolve. You become increasingly self-sufficient over time.

**Who this is for:**
- You're curious about how AI works under the hood
- You want to be able to request changes and understand what's happening
- You plan to scale your use of AI across your business over time
- You might want to build some automations yourself eventually

**What you get:**
- Everything from the Done-For-You path
- Guided walkthroughs of each layer as it's built
- Training on voice-first AI interaction
- A growing understanding of what's possible — so you keep finding new things to automate

---

## WILBA's Recommendation

Start with Done-For-You. As you get comfortable and see results, you'll naturally start asking "could it also do X?" — and that's when the Guided Path kicks in. Most clients evolve from one to the other naturally.
