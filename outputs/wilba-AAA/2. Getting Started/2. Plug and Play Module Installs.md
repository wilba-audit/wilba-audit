# Plug & Play Module Installs

**Every module works the same way. Consistent, repeatable, reliable.**

AIOS modules are pre-built systems packaged for instant deployment. Each one adds a new capability to your workspace — a data pipeline, an intelligence layer, a communication bot, a content system. They're designed to snap in without breaking anything that's already working.

---

## The Install Flow

Every module follows the same pattern:

1. **Identify the need** — What's eating your time? What data do you need? What process is manual?
2. **Select the module** — Match the problem to a proven module from the library
3. **Install it** — The module gets dropped into your workspace and configured for your specific business
4. **Customise** — AI asks questions about your setup, your tools, your preferences — then adapts the module to fit
5. **Validate** — Test everything. Make sure it works with your real data, your real workflows

That's it. Same every time. Whether it's the first module or the tenth.

---

## What to Expect During Install

Modules aren't just "click and done." They're consultative — AI acts like a consultant who's setting up a system for your specific business:

- **Conversations and interviews** — AI asks about your data sources, your content pipeline, your team structure
- **API connections** — Some modules need access to your tools (Stripe, Google Analytics, your booking system). We walk you through each one
- **Customisation** — Every module adapts to YOUR business. No two installs are identical
- **Testing** — Nothing goes live without validation. We test with your real data before anything touches your operations
