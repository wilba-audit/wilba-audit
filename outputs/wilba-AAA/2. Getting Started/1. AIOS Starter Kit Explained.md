# AIOS Starter Kit Explained

**Before we build anything, here's what you're getting.**

Every AIOS starts from the same foundation: the Starter Kit. It's a pre-built workspace structure that AI reads and understands. Think of it as the skeleton of your AI Operating System — the bones that everything else attaches to.

---

## The Big Picture

Your AIOS workspace is a folder on your computer (or server). Inside that folder is a set of files and directories that AI reads at the start of every session. These files tell AI who you are, what your business does, what your priorities are, and what tools are available. The better these files are, the smarter your AI becomes.

The Starter Kit gives you this folder structure out of the box — with templates, commands, and instructions already in place.

---

## What's Inside

### CLAUDE.md — The Foundation
The most important file in your entire workspace. It's automatically loaded at the start of every AI session. It tells AI:
- What this workspace is
- What your AIOS mission is
- What the folder structure looks like
- What commands are available
- How AI should behave (patient, context-aware, proactive)

As modules get installed and the workspace grows, CLAUDE.md grows with it. It's a living document — the single source of truth.

### context/ — Your Business Brain
This folder holds everything AI needs to know about you and your business:
- **business-info.md** — What your business does, your services, your market
- **personal-info.md** — Who you are, your role, your background
- **strategy.md** — Current priorities, goals, what you're focused on
- **current-data.md** — Key metrics, current state of the business
- **import/** — Drop any documents here for AI to analyse

### module-installs/ — Plug & Play Modules
Pre-built capabilities that snap into your workspace. Each one adds a new layer — data pipelines, intelligence collection, automation tools.

### scripts/ — Automation Scripts
The actual code that powers your automations. Python scripts, cron jobs, webhooks — all managed by AI but living here for transparency.

### outputs/ — Deliverables
Everything your AIOS produces — reports, analyses, briefs, content — organised by type and purpose.

### plans/ — Implementation Plans
Detailed plans for new features or changes. Created before building, executed step by step.
