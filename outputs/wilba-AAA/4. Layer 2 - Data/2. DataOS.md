# DataOS

**Centralise your business data. Build your dashboard. Stop logging into 7 platforms every morning.**

---

## What This Is

DataOS is an interactive data workshop. We interview you about your data sources — where do your numbers live? What matters most? What do you check every day? — then build custom collectors for each one. Everything flows into a single database. We generate a key-metrics summary, build you a custom dashboard, and set up daily automated collection.

**Six phases:** Discovery (brainstorm your data sources) → Foundation (database setup) → Connect Sources (build collectors one by one) → Key Metrics (auto-generated summary) → Dashboard Workshop (build your visual dashboard) → Automate (schedule daily collection).

---

## Why This Matters

Right now your data is scattered across 10 different dashboards. You check Stripe here, GA4 there, your CRM somewhere else. Every morning starts with a tour of browser tabs just to know what's going on.

DataOS centralises everything so AI sees the full picture — and so do you. The custom dashboard gives you a single view of your business. The daily collection means your numbers are always fresh. And because it's all in a queryable database, you can ask AI anything: *"Compare this month to last month." "What's my conversion rate trending?" "Which channel drives the most revenue?"*

This is the data foundation that feeds your Intelligence layer (Layer 3), your Daily Brief, and every data-driven decision your AIOS helps you make.
