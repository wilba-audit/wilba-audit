# InfraOS

**Version control, backups, documentation, and security. The skeleton that keeps your AIOS safe and organised as it grows.**

---

## What This Is

InfraOS sets up Git version control (explained for complete beginners), connects your workspace to cloud backup, installs a merged commit command that saves and documents your work in one step, and creates a documentation system that grows with your workspace. It also configures your environment with the API keys needed for future modules.

---

## Why This Matters

You just built your context layer — that's valuable work. InfraOS protects it. Version control means every change is tracked and reversible. If something breaks, you roll back. If you want to see what changed last week, you check the log. Cloud backup means your entire AIOS is safe even if your machine dies.

Think of ContextOS as the brain of your AIOS. InfraOS is the skeleton — the structure that holds everything together and keeps it safe.

---

## What You'll Have After

- Git version control initialised
- Cloud backup connected — your workspace safe and sound
- A commit command that saves, documents, and backs up in one step
- Change history tracking every modification
- Security practices that protect your credentials and sensitive data
