# ContextOS

**The foundation of your entire AIOS. Without this, AI is generic. With this, AI is YOUR strategic partner.**

---

## What This Is

ContextOS is a guided process where AI learns about your business across four areas — what your business does, who you are, your strategy, and your current data. It builds structured context files from your answers and creates a personalised knowledge base that gets loaded at the start of every session.

Three ways to provide context: import existing documents (business plans, strategy docs, proposals), guided interview (AI asks, you answer), or paste text. Most businesses use a mix.

---

## Why This Matters

This is the single most important setup step. The quality of your context directly determines the quality of everything AI does for you. Every module after this builds on this foundation. Without it, AI is the same as everyone else's. With it, AI knows your team names, your revenue targets, your current priorities — and every conversation starts from that informed position.

Think of it this way: you're giving AI the same briefing you'd give a new executive hire on their first day. Except AI never forgets it.
