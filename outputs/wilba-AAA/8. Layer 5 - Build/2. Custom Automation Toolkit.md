# Custom Automation Toolkit

**Two commands that give you a structured way to go from "I want to automate X" to a working system.**

---

## What This Is

The Custom Automation Toolkit adds structured commands to your workspace designed specifically for custom building:

- **/brainstorm** — For when you don't know where to start. It reads your Task Audit, your business context, and your data. Helps you figure out what's possible, what's worth automating, generates options and approaches.
- **/explore** — For when you have a specific idea. Interactive back-and-forth across 5 stages: Discovery → Research → Shape → Scope → Output. Produces a scoped exploration document you can turn into a plan and then execute.

---

## Why This Matters

Building custom automations without a structured process leads to scope creep, wasted time, and abandoned projects. You start excited, get 30% in, realise you didn't think something through, and give up.

These commands prevent that by giving you a disciplined workflow:
1. **/brainstorm** — figure out WHAT to build
2. **/explore** — shape the idea, research feasibility, define scope
3. **/create-plan** — turn the exploration into a step-by-step plan
4. **/implement** — execute the plan chunk by chunk
