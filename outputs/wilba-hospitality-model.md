# WILBA — The Hospitality Model
## The wedge, the offer, and the retreat partnership. This is the business.

**The model in one line:**
> Build AIOS for hospitality properties — sold as a **marketing/growth package** — then **partner with those same properties as venues** to run retreats for your founder audience.

**Why this is the one:** it's a single niche where every part feeds the others, it's productizable, and it's *your actual world* (surf, yoga, lifestyle, Baha Baha). Hormozi says niche the starving crowd; Ottley says niche down. This does both — and adds a moat (retreats) nobody else in AI has.

---

## Part 1 — The offer: sell it as MARKETING, not "AIOS"

Hospitality owners don't buy "an AI Operating System." They buy **more bookings, fewer OTA commissions, and less admin.** So we wrap the AIOS in language they already reach for their wallet for.

**Working names:** "Booked Out" · "Full House" · "The Direct Booking Engine" · "The Hospitality Growth System"

**What's inside (all AIOS under the hood, sold as outcomes):**
| They hear | It actually is |
|---|---|
| "Never miss a booking again" | AI guest agent — WhatsApp/email/IG, 24/7, multilingual |
| "Run your property in 10 min a day" | Daily ops brief / to-do list |
| "Stop paying Booking.com 20%" | Direct-booking engine — Mailchimp + QR + rebooking nurture |
| "Stay booked all year" | Social autopilot + content |
| "Get found when people ask AI where to stay" | GEO/LLM discoverability |

**The pitch:** *"I fill your rooms and run your front desk on autopilot — so you get your life back and keep more of every booking."*

**Money model:** setup fee ($3–8k, productized so delivery is fast) + monthly retainer ($400–1,500) + the retreat revenue layer (below). Land with one piece (the guest agent), expand to the full system. Baha Baha = pilot #1.

---

## Part 2 — The retreat partnership (the moat + the magic)

You run **your retreats** (for your founder/AIOS-builder audience) **at the properties you've automated.** Everyone wins three ways:

- **The property gets:** their AIOS/marketing built + **guaranteed room revenue** (you fill their off-season) + content + exposure to your audience.
- **You get:** a stunning venue with **zero capital outlay**, a premium offer ($2–5k+/head), a warm partner who already trusts you, and a filming location that *is* the brand.
- **Your guests get:** "come to a surf paradise, build your AI business, surf, yoga, sauna, ice bath, leave with it running."

**Partnership economics:** because you're already their marketing partner, you get favourable venue rates (or rev-share on rooms). They'd rather fill empty rooms at cost than leave them empty.

**The killer B2B pitch to any property:** *"I'll automate your bookings AND bring you a retreat that fills your quiet season."* That's almost impossible to say no to.

## Part 3 — Why it's a flywheel

```
Build AIOS for a property (cash + case study)
        │

        │
        ▼
Run a founder retreat there → premium revenue + deep relationships + content
        │

Retreat guests + content → more founders in your audience/community
        │
        ▼
Some become AIOS clients · properties refer other properties
        │

```

**The unlock:** every hospitality property you automate is *also* a future retreat venue. The done-for-you work builds your retreat infrastructure for free.

## Part 5 — Start narrow (the discipline)
Don't launch retreats yet. Sequence:
1. **Nail the hospitality package with 2–3 properties** (Baha Baha first) → cash + case studies + proof the productized build works.
2. **Build the audience** in parallel (content — surf-coast-founder building AI for beautiful properties).
3. **Run the first retreat** at Baha Baha once you've got the proof, the partner, and enough audience to fill it.

Near-term cash (the builds) literally funds and de-risks the big vision (retreats).
