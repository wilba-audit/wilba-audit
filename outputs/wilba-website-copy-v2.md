---

### process (numbered, stacked vertically — clean and scannable)

**01 — onboard**
a 30-minute call to learn your brand, your audience, and your voice. you'll record a short clip so we can build your ai avatar. that's your only on-camera moment.

**02 — research**
our ai analyses trending topics, top-performing content, and audience signals in your niche. we build a strategy designed to grow your authority — not just your follower count.

**03 — script**
every post gets a hook that stops the scroll, a message worth watching, and a call-to-action that converts. written in your tone. reviewed by you.

**04 — produce**
your ai avatar delivers each script on camera. we edit, add captions, music, and graphics. every video is formatted for reels, shorts, tiktok, and linkedin.

**05 — publish**
we schedule across your platforms at optimal times. you get monthly performance insights. we optimise. the cycle continues.

---
