## 1. What the winning creators actually do (the research)

The current playbook among top AI/Claude-Code creators on Instagram (mid-2026):

1. **Generosity is the marketing.** They give away genuinely useful skills, agents, prompts, and hacks — for free. The perceived leader is whoever gives away the most usable stuff. You don't sell in the content; you *demonstrate value so obviously* that people chase the link.
2. **The comment-to-DM funnel.** A high-reach Reel ends with a keyword CTA — *"comment SKILLS and I'll send it to you."* An automation (Manychat / Instagram-native) auto-DMs the lead a link. One documented case: **2,500+ leads from a single Reel.**
3. **The skills sheet is the lead magnet.** The link goes to a "skills sheet" — a Notion/Google doc listing ready-to-use Claude Code skills/agents with what each does. It captures an email in exchange.
4. **The Skool is the money.** Email nurtures into a paid community (Skool) for people building their own AI systems. Pricing typically **$50–70/month**. Top AI Skools do **$50k–$300k/month**. A $10k/mo Skool = ~150–200 members at $55–65.
5. **Format skews to Reels:** screen-recorded build-in-public, "steal this" hacks, and talking-head authority. Hook-Substance-Payoff structure. Fast, real, unpolished-but-sharp.

**Sources:** @ai_with_albert (Instagram); Sabrina Ramonov (sabrina.dev — AI business + Skool monetization); creatorflow.so, mindstudio.ai, stormy.ai on Claude Code content skills; 2026 Instagram lead-gen guides on comment-to-DM funnels.
