# Monkey Joe's — Weekly Campaign Templates
### Two Templates. Swap the Hook Each Week. 30 Minutes and Done.

**Prepared by:** Jess Morrell / WILBA
**Platform:** GoHighLevel
**Cadence:** 2 sends per week, every week

---

## HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

Each week you send two campaigns from GoHighLevel:

| Send | Day + Time | Template |
|---|---|---|
| **Campaign 1** | Tuesday evening (5–7pm) | Wednesday BOGO Push |
| **Campaign 2** | Friday morning (8–10am) | Weekend Preview + Birthday Party |

**What you change each week:** The hook (first line or subject line variation). Everything else stays the same or near the same. Total time: ~30 minutes per week for both.

**Segment:** Send to all **Active** contacts (opened/clicked in last 90 days). Do NOT send to Lapsed until they've been through the reactivation sequence first.

## Weekly Checklist (Takes 30 Minutes Total)

Before sending each week:

**Tuesday (Campaign 1 — Wednesday BOGO):**
- [ ] Pick subject line from rotation (don't repeat last week's)
- [ ] Pick hook from rotation
- [ ] Confirm Wednesday hours are accurate
- [ ] Check if any rain in forecast — if yes, use rainy day hook
- [ ] Send test to yourself first
- [ ] Schedule for 5–7pm Tuesday in GoHighLevel
- [ ] Confirm segment: Active contacts only

**Friday (Campaign 2 — Weekend Preview):**
