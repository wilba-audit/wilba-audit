## THE BRAND FOUNDATION

### The One Idea

> **"You get to breathe. They get the best time of their life."**

This is the through-line. Every piece of copy in this document serves this dual promise.

- The **parent** feels: relief, permission to relax, being understood
- The **child** gets: magic, chaos, joy, the best time
- The **venue** delivers: both — simultaneously — and has for 21 years

### Voice

Warm. A little cheeky. Like your funniest parent friend who happened to find the best deal in town. Never corporate. Never over-polished. Always real.

**The test for every line:** Would a tired mum of two smile and nod reading this? If yes, keep it. If it sounds like a press release, rewrite it.

### Writing Rules

- Short sentences. One idea per line.
- Talk to the parent: "you," "your kid," never generic
- Never use a child's name — privacy (parents signed the waiver on behalf of minors)
- Emoji: 1–2 max, only where they add warmth (not decoration)
- Subject lines: 6 words or fewer
- CTAs: verb + specific outcome ("Claim your free admission" not "Click here")
- Numbers are trust: "21 years" not "decades of experience," "$5" not "affordable pricing"
