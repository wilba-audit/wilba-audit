## COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE SUMMARY

Use this when briefing ad copy or landing page messaging:

| Competitor | Est. Child Price | Adults | Walk-In | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Monkey Joe's** | $12.99–$13.99 | **FREE** | **Yes** | Our advantages bolded |
| Kidiverse | ~$20.49 | Varies | Varies | More expensive, older-skewing |
| Funtastic Depot | $14.95–$24.95 | Included | Yes | Live DJ weekends, more variety |
| Pump It Up | ~$9 open play | Included | **No — must book** | Private-event model, less flexible |
| Rebounderz | ~$20/hr | Included | Yes | Trampolines = older kids |
| Sky Zone | $19–$28/hr | Included | Yes | Expensive, older demographic |
| Launch Entertainment | $18–$25 | Included | Yes | Laser tag/bowling added |
| Millie Moo's | Varies | Included | Yes | Under-5 specialist |

**The two things that win us every comparison:**
1. **Adults free** — at most competitors, a family of 2 parents + 2 kids costs $40–$60 just to get in. At Monkey Joe's it's $25–$28.
2. **No reservations required** — Pump It Up's biggest weakness is their requirement to book. We win every impulse decision.

**Lead with these in every ad, every landing page, every email subject line.**

## BIDDING STRATEGY

**Launch settings (Week 1–4):**
- Strategy: Maximize Clicks
- CPC cap: $3.00 (prevents overspending while learning)
- Adjust down to $2.50 after week 1 if data shows lower CPCs are winning

**After 30 conversions tracked (approx. Month 2):**
- Switch to Target CPA bidding
- Set initial target CPA: $15 for open play, $35 for birthday party inquiry
- These are starting targets — adjust based on actual results

**Bid adjustments:**
| Adjustment | Setting |
|---|---|
| Mobile | +25% (most searches on phone) |
| Friday–Sunday | +20% (higher intent, families planning weekend/next week) |
| 11am–7pm weekdays | +20% (parents planning same-day or next-day visits) |
| 6am–10am weekdays | Standard |
| 10pm–6am | -50% or turn off (very low conversion) |
