# CAMPAIGN OFFER B: 50% OFF
## Half Price Admissions — Ages 3 to 12, Weekdays

**The pitch:** Same emotional buy-in as BOGO, different framing — works for families with one kid AND families with more. 50% off feels concrete and trustworthy. Easy to understand, hard to ignore.

**Emotional hook:** Great day, half the price. The math is obvious. The decision is easy.

**Why test this against BOGO:** Some families don't have two kids. "Buy one get one" requires two. "50% off" applies to everyone. We're testing whether broader eligibility = more conversions, or whether BOGO's "get one FREE" framing is simply more irresistible.

**Audience:** Parents of children ages 3–12, 10-mile radius, ages 25–45, Facebook + Instagram
**Days:** Weekdays only (Mon–Fri)
**Code:** `KIDS50-POL` (Pointe Orlando) / `KIDS50-WP` (Winter Park)
