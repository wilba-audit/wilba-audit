10 — How we measure

Judge authority, not vanity.

Leading indicators (we control)

  • Posting consistency
  • Hook test rate
  • Saves + shares per post
  • Average watch %
  • Email list growth
  • DM / comment quality

Lagging indicators (outcomes)

  • Profile → follow %
  • Lead-magnet signups
  • Consult enquiries from social
  • Branded search (“Dr Gina Schoeman”)
  • Booked consults
No follower-count promise. That’s a vanity trap. We commit to a system and the leading indicators we can actually move — precise 90-day targets set once her baseline analytics are in hand.

11 — Non-negotiable

Trust is the asset. We protect it.

Educational framing over promotional. No unverified claims — cite evidence, acknowledge nuance (that is her differentiator vs. hype-sellers). Patient stories only where GMC/ASA-permitted, anonymised and consented. On BHRT: precise about bioidentical vs body-identical, safety, and licensed vs. compounded. Precision reads as authority here.
