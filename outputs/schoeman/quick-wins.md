# Dr Gina Schoeman — Quick Wins: Leverage the Videos She Already Paid For

**Prepared by:** WILBA (Jess Morrell) · **Date:** 2026-07-24
**Premise:** The videos are high quality but under-leveraged. None of the wins
below require new filming or new spend — they extract the value already sitting
in her existing footage, and fix the distribution gaps proven in her real data.

---

## The expert read (why this works)
Production value doesn't buy reach — retention and shares do. So the fastest
path isn't *more/better* videos; it's making the great footage she **already
owns** work 10× harder. Right now each video does one job (one post) and stops.
We turn each one into a *campaign*. This is the highest ROI move available to
her, because the expensive part (filming a credible expert) is already done.

## The three leaks we're plugging
1. **No atomization** — 1 video → 1 post (should be 1 video → ~10 assets).
2. **No hook** — great footage, weak first 2 seconds → scrolled.
3. **Broken distribution** — [REAL] from her LinkedIn: 2 fragmented profiles,
   burst posting (30–37× view swings), ~0 reposts. Nothing compounds.

---

## Quick wins, ranked by speed × impact (all use existing footage)

### ① Atomize the library into hook-first clips ★ biggest win
Take each existing long video and cut **3–6 short clips** (20–40s), each teaching
ONE idea. A handful of professional videos becomes **20–30 posts.** Zero new spend.

### ② Re-engineer the first 2 seconds
Open each clip on the payoff/tension with an **on-screen text promise** in frame 1
("Your 'normal' bloods are hiding this"). Cut the intros, logos, and slow set-ups.
Same footage, multiples of the watch-through.

### ③ Burn in captions
~85% watch muted. If the clips don't have bold on-screen captions, adding them is
a near-instant retention lift. Fast and mechanical.

### ④ Repurpose each clip natively across platforms
1 clip → **IG Reel + TikTok + LinkedIn native video + YouTube Short.** Post
*natively* (upload the file), never as a link. One cut, 4 placements.

### ⑤ Turn each video's key points into a document carousel
Pull the 3–5 teaching points from a video into a swipeable carousel — the highest
save/share format on both LinkedIn and Instagram. Screenshot-able authority.

### ⑥ Consolidate LinkedIn + commit to a rhythm
From her data: pick ONE primary profile, retire/redirect the other, set the
company page to reshare. Then **3 posts/week, fixed days** — ends the 30× volatility.

### ⑦ Pin the best clip + open a conversion path
Pin her single strongest clip to each profile. Add ONE CTA → a simple lead magnet
("Is It Your Hormones? 27-symptom checklist"), link in the **first comment** on
LinkedIn (in-body links suppress reach).

### ⑧ Spend 15 min/day in the comments
Reply thoughtfully on peers', journalists', and patients' posts. This is how she
breaks past the network ceiling her low Reach numbers show.

## The 14-day quick-win sprint

**Days 1–2 — Set up**
- Gather all existing video files. Pick the 6 strongest.
- Consolidate LinkedIn to one profile; set company page to reshare. `[CONFIRM primary]`
- Stand up the lead magnet + email capture.

**Days 3–5 — Manufacture the content bank**
- Cut **18–24 hook-first clips** from the 6 videos, with burned-in captions.
- Draft hook + caption for each (payoff-first).

**Days 6–7 — Add the share-drivers**
- Build **4 document carousels** from the videos' key points.

**Week 2 — Distribute consistently**
- Post **3×/week** on IG + LinkedIn (native), mirror to TikTok/Shorts.
- Pin the best clip + lead-magnet CTA. 15 min/day commenting.
- Track **saves, shares/reposts, profile→follow** — not view spikes.
