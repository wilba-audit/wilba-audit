Authority compounds; it doesn't spike overnight, and anyone who promises "viral"
is guessing. Here's the honest arc:
- **Months 1–3:** foundations fixed — profile, one clear audience, a hook
  system, a real cadence, YouTube as your evergreen moat, and an email list you
  own. First signs: watch-time up, saves and shares up, better DMs.
- **Months 3–6:** consistent, qualified reach and the first patient enquiries
  you can trace to social.
- **Months 6–12:** it compounds — social becomes a reliable, low-cost channel
  feeding your premium consults.
