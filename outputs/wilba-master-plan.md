## 1. The honest headline (read this twice)

You have **two revenue engines**, and they move at different speeds:

| Engine | What it is | Speed to $10k | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| **🏦 The Agency** | Warm outreach → free audit → done-for-you AI (receptionist / automation / hospitality) | **Fast — this is your 60-day $10k** | Pays the bills NOW |
| **🔁 The Builder Brand → Skool** | Content → skills sheet → Skool community for AIOS builds | **Slow — 3-6 months to real money** | Compounds into recurring income |

**The mistake to avoid:** betting your 60-day rent money on the Skool. A community from a cold start doesn't hit $10k in 8 weeks — anyone who tells you otherwise is selling a course.

**The truth:** your $10k in 60 days comes from the **agency**. The **Skool** is the empire you build in parallel — and the content that feeds it also makes your agency closes easier (credibility) and gives you case studies. They feed each other. But they are not the same timeline.

## 2. How your week actually splits (ruthless priority)

Of your working time:
- **~60% → Agency outreach + sales.** This is the cash. Warm network first. (See the sprint plan.)
- **~25% → Content.** One film session a week + posting. Builds the brand + seeds the Skool. I finish everything.
- **~15% → Funnel + Skool setup.** One-time, front-loaded in the first 2 weeks, then near-zero.

If a day is ever a choice between "make content" and "do outreach" — **do outreach.** Content compounds; outreach pays.

## 3. The plan in four phases

### Phase 0 — Turn on the machines (this week)
- 📸 Send me your photo → I build your covers/thumbnails

- 🔗 Skills sheet gets a home + email capture
- 💬 Manychat connected (keyword SKILLS)

### Phase 1 — Cash sprint + content rhythm (weeks 1-4)
- **Agency:** 10-12 warm outreach touches/day → audits → calls → first closes. *(This is the priority.)*
- **Content:** post 4-5 reels/week from your weekly film session. Comment-to-DM live. Skills sheet collecting emails.
- **Skool:** open a free 2-week beta to your first ~20 DMs. Get them a win. Collect testimonials.
- 🎯 **Target: first 1-2 agency closes, first cash in, Skool seeded.**

### Phase 2 — Close the $10k + switch the Skool on (weeks 5-8)
- **Agency:** push the pipeline to close. ~5 total closes = your $10k cash month. Every happy client = a case study → content.
- **Content:** keep the weekly rhythm. Double down on whatever hook overperformed.
- **Skool:** flip on founding-member pricing ($39, first 50). Email nurture running from the skills sheet.
- 🎯 **Target: $10k cash-collected month + $2-4k MRR building underneath (agency retainers + first Skool members).**

### Phase 3 — The Skool becomes the engine (month 3+)
- Agency gives you cash + credibility + case studies.
- The builder brand has an audience now; the Skool grows toward $10k recurring on its own.
- You shift more time from outreach → content + community as recurring income replaces done-for-you hustle.
- 🎯 **Target: recurring revenue (retainers + Skool) covers your number without constant selling.**

## 4. Where the $10k actually comes from (60 days)

**Agency — your bill-payer (illustrative route to $10k cash collected):**
- 3 × AI Receptionist ($2,000 setup + $400/mo) = $6,000 + $1,200 MRR
- 2 × Content Machine or Hospitality Stack ($2,000-2,500 setup + retainer) = ~$4,500 + ~$1,800 MRR
- ≈ **$10.5k collected + ~$3k MRR** from ~5 warm closes.

**Skool — your compounding engine (60-day trajectory, NOT the $10k):**
- Beta → founding members → maybe $1-3k MRR by day 60. Then it climbs toward $10k over months 3-6 as the audience grows.

**The number to obsess over:** ~10-12 warm outreach touches a day. That single habit produces the ~5 closes that hit $10k. Content and Skool are the flywheel around it.
