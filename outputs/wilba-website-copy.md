### Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1: Onboarding & Voice Capture
We start with a short call to understand your brand, audience, and content goals. You'll record a quick 2-minute video so we can build your AI avatar and voice profile.

*Time investment from you: ~30 minutes total.*

#### Step 2: Trend Research & Strategy
Our AI scans trending topics, top-performing posts, and relevant industry news in your niche. We build a monthly content strategy designed to grow your authority and attract your ideal clients.

*Delivered: Content calendar, topic list, and strategic direction.*

#### Step 3: Script Generation
For each post, we craft a hook, a value-packed middle, and a clear call-to-action — all written in your tone. You review the scripts and suggest any changes.

*What you see: Clean, conversational scripts ready to film or feed into your avatar.*

#### Step 4: Avatar Video Creation
Your approved scripts are fed into our AI video engine. Your digital avatar delivers each script on camera — matching your voice, face, and mannerisms.

*What you get: Raw talking-head videos, ready for editing.*

#### Step 5: Editing & Packaging (Tier 3)
We add captions, music, transitions, B-roll, and on-brand graphics. Every video is formatted for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and LinkedIn.

*What you get: Polished, ready-to-post videos.*

#### Step 6: Scheduling & Insights (Tier 3)
We schedule your content across all platforms at optimal times. At the end of each month, you get a performance report with insights and recommendations for the next cycle.
