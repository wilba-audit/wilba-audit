## The core sequence: The 3 Cs — Capabilities → Contact → Convert

Most people skip straight to Convert (trying to sell). That's why nothing works. You do them in order.

### 1. Capabilities
Know what AI can *actually* do — deeply enough to spot where it fits inside a real business. Not "ChatGPT writes emails." Generative AI, agents, workflows, computer vision, NLP, data pipelines. If your understanding is surface-level in a room with someone who'd pay $10–20k, you've lost before you start.
> **Jess already has this.** She builds real AIOS systems. Capability is not her bottleneck — Contact and Convert are.

### 2. Contact — get in front of humans. Four channels:
- **Warm outreach** — highest-converting path to your first client. Old colleagues, friends, family, your dentist, mechanic, local business owners. People who already trust you. **Start here.**
- **In person** — trust is easier face-to-face. Events, coffee shops, casual conversations.
- **Cold outreach** — works *only* with research. Understand the industry, business, and person first. **Don't sell the build — sell the meeting.**
- **Content** — the long game. Pick ONE platform and show up. (LinkedIn easiest to start; that's how Morningside began.)

### 3. Convert — the first call is NOT a pitch
- Your job is to **understand**, not sell. Ask questions. Find the bottlenecks. Talk outcomes. Make them feel like *finally, someone gets it.*
- If they know exactly what they want built → **quote it.**
- If they don't (most) → the offer isn't the build, **it's the audit.**
- A good audit opens the door to **the build → the retainer → the next department.** (Land and expand.)

---

## The principles underneath it
- **Stop chasing tools. Focus on landing a client.** Action over perfection.
- **Niche down.** One industry + one service. Specific beats broad. "AI for X" outsells "AI for anyone."
- **Sell outcomes, not tech.** Nobody buys "an AI agent." They buy more bookings, less admin, hours back.
- **Case study first.** Do one build cheap/free for proof, then charge full price with the testimonial.
- **Land and expand.** Start with one automation → become the AI person for the whole business.
- **Speed wins.** The person who acts today beats the person with the better plan next month.
- **Pricing:** setup fee + monthly retainer. Full AIOS builds command $10–30k+. Start where you can close, raise with proof.
