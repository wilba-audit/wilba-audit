# WILBA.ai Launch Plan (March 2026)

> Source: Research document provided by Jess, March 2026

---

## 1. Business Overview

**Core product:** AI-powered content generation machine for coaches, consultants and service-based entrepreneurs.

**Pipeline:** Perplexity (research) → Script → ElevenLabs (voice) → HeyGen (avatar video) → CreatorMate (scheduling)

**Target audience:** Coaches, consultants, small-business owners wanting authority on Instagram without a creative team.

### Service Tiers

| Tier | What's Included | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| Tier 1 – Strategy & Script | Trend analysis + content strategy + hook + script | Client films themselves |
| Tier 2 – Script & Avatar Video | Tier 1 + AI avatar talking-head video | Client edits/downloads raw footage |
| Tier 3 – Full Production & Scheduling | Tier 2 + editing, captions, music, B-roll + scheduling | Turnkey solution |

## 2. Recommended Tools

**Website:** Wix (Jess has subscription + wilba.ai domain)

**AI Avatar:**
- HeyGen — hyper-realistic talking-head, 1,100+ stock avatars, 175 languages *(current tool in pipeline)*
- Synthesia — best for full-body, professional grade (takes days to create custom avatar)
- D-ID — still photo → talking head, head/shoulders only

**Video Editing:**
- CapCut — best for short-form vertical video, auto-captions, fast
- Runway — creative effects, text-to-video, object removal
- Pictory — repurpose long-form content into clips

## 5. Content Generation Workflow (Internal SOP)

1. **Trend research** — Google Trends, Exploding Topics, Instagram search, AI summarisation
2. **Script generation** — Hook (1-2 sec grab) → Value → CTA. AI draft → human edit for tone.
3. **Avatar creation** — HeyGen/Synthesia. Upload script, select voice/gestures, review output.
4. **Video editing** — CapCut (speed), Runway (creative), Pictory (repurposing)
5. **Scheduling** — Later, Buffer or Meta Business Suite. Monitor: views, watch time, saves, shares.

---

## 6. Instagram Trends (2026)

- Micro-drama + educational storytelling > viral dances
- Authenticity over polish — behind-the-scenes, imperfections welcome
- Hook in first 1.5 seconds → value → clear CTA
- Engagement depth (saves/shares) > likes
- Instagram SEO: keywords in captions, alt text, bio
